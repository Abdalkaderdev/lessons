# Fares's Curriculum (Age 10)

## Goal
Discover what AI is through fun, visual, and interactive activities — no coding pressure, just curiosity.

---

## Module 1: AI for Studying

Use AI as your personal tutor and study partner. Learn how to ask better questions, understand hard topics, quiz yourself, and explore anything you're curious about.

| # | Lesson | What You'll Do |
|---|--------|----------------|
| 1 | [AI Homework Helper](./ai-for-studying/01-ai-homework-helper.md) | Ask AI to explain hard topics simply; use the "explain it back" trick |
| 2 | [Quiz Yourself with AI](./ai-for-studying/02-quiz-yourself.md) | Get AI to make quizzes on any topic — multiple choice, true/false, rapid fire |
| 3 | [Learn Anything with AI](./ai-for-studying/03-learn-anything.md) | Explore space, dinosaurs, science, history — go deep on topics you love |

---

## Module 2: AI for Fun

Play games, write stories, and create art — all with AI as your creative partner.

| # | Lesson | What You'll Do |
|---|--------|----------------|
| 1 | [Story Time with AI](./ai-for-fun/01-story-time.md) | Write adventure stories, interactive choose-your-own-path tales, and comic ideas |
| 2 | [Riddles, Puzzles & Games](./ai-for-fun/02-riddles-and-games.md) | Play 20 Questions, trivia battles, riddles, and invent your own games |
| 3 | [AI Art & Creativity](./ai-for-fun/03-ai-art-and-music.md) | Write image prompts, design characters, write song lyrics and poems |

---

## Module 3: AI for Creativity

Think like a designer, storyteller, and inventor. Build your own world and your own invention — with AI by your side.

| # | Lesson | What You'll Do |
|---|--------|----------------|
| 1 | [Design Your Own World](./ai-for-creativity/01-design-your-world.md) | Build a game world, fantasy land, or future city — map, characters, and history |
| 2 | [Be an Inventor](./ai-for-creativity/02-be-an-inventor.md) | Spot a real problem, invent a solution, and pitch it Shark Tank style |

---

## Learning Path

```
Module 1: AI for Studying  -->  Module 2: AI for Fun  -->  Module 3: AI for Creativity
(3 lessons)                      (3 lessons)                  (2 lessons)
```

Each lesson takes about 30-45 minutes. No coding. No pressure. Just exploration and creativity!

---

## Tips for Fares

- There are no wrong answers — just ideas to explore
- If something confuses you, ask AI to explain it differently
- The best prompts are the ones you make up yourself
- Every lesson has a Homework section — try to do it before the next session!
