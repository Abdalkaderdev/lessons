# Fares's Curriculum (Age 10)

## Goal
Discover what AI is through fun, visual, and interactive activities — no coding pressure, just curiosity.

## Modules

### Module 1: What is AI?
- AI is like a super-smart helper
- Things that use AI (Siri, YouTube recommendations, video games)
- AI vs humans — what AI can and can't do

### Module 2: Talking to AI
- Using Claude to chat, ask questions, and get help with homework
- Fun prompts: ask Claude to write a story, explain dinosaurs, or create a quiz
- Learning to ask good questions

### Module 3: AI and Art
- Using AI to generate images and ideas
- Creating stories with Claude
- Designing a character or a game idea with AI help

### Module 4: How Does AI Learn?
- Simple explanation of how AI learns from examples
- Playing pattern-matching games
- Understanding that AI makes mistakes too

### Module 5: AI in Everyday Life
- AI in games, phones, cars, and robots
- Cool AI projects for kids
- Thinking about the future with AI

### Module 6: My First Webpage (Fun Version)
- Simple drag-and-drop website builders
- Making a fun page about a topic Fares loves
- Sharing it with family
