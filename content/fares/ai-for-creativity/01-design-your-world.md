# 🌏 Design Your Own World

### What if YOU got to design the map, the rules, and the characters? Let's build a world!

---

## What You'll Learn

- How to use AI to design a game world, fantasy land, or future city
- How to create characters with detailed backstories
- How to use AI to generate visual ideas for your drawings
- How to turn your world into a mini presentation or project

---

## Every Great Story Starts with a World

Think about your favorite games, movies, or books. Before any of the action happened, someone had to build the WORLD.

Someone decided:
- What the land looks like
- What kinds of people (or creatures) live there
- What the rules are
- What the history is
- What the conflicts are

That person is a **world builder**. And now, with AI as your partner, you can be one too.

Whether it's a fantasy kingdom, a space civilization, a future city, or a game level — you're the architect. AI is your assistant.

---

## Step 1: Choose Your World Type

Before you start designing, pick what kind of world excites you most:

**Fantasy World** — Magic, dragons, castles, kingdoms, heroes
**Sci-Fi World** — Space, robots, advanced technology, alien planets
**Future Earth** — Earth in 100 or 500 years, what changed?
**Game World** — A world designed for a specific game (platformer, RPG, strategy)
**Underwater World** — Civilizations under the sea
**Microscopic World** — What if tiny things were huge? Life inside a computer?

You can mix and match! A "fantasy world on a distant planet" is totally valid.

---

## Step 2: Build the Basics

Start with the big picture. Ask AI to help you fill in the core elements:

> Prompt: "I want to build a fantasy world called Emberfall. It's a land where fire magic is the most powerful force. There are 3 kingdoms that are always at war over control of a giant volcano. Help me flesh out this world — give me:
> - A description of each kingdom and its culture
> - What people look like in each kingdom
> - What the landscape is like
> - One unique rule or law in each kingdom"

Write down what you like. Cross out what you don't. This is YOUR world — you're the boss!

---

## Step 3: Design the Map

Every great world needs a map. Ask AI to describe what your world looks like:

> Prompt: "Describe the geography of my world, Emberfall. Where is the volcano? Where are the 3 kingdoms? Are there mountains, forests, deserts, oceans? What are the most dangerous places? Describe it so I could draw a map."

Now you can actually DRAW your map! AI gives you the description, you translate it into a drawing. You don't have to be a great artist — a simple sketch with labels is amazing.

---

## Step 4: Create Characters

A world is nothing without the people (or creatures) in it. Let's build some characters.

**The Hero:**
> Prompt: "Create a main character for my world Emberfall. She's a 14-year-old girl who discovered she has ice magic — which is extremely rare and feared in a fire-magic world. Give her:
> - A name
> - A personality (3 traits)
> - A backstory (where she came from, what happened to her family)
> - What she wants most
> - What she's afraid of
> - A unique skill or ability"

**The Villain:**
> Prompt: "Create the main villain for Emberfall. They used to be good but turned evil after a tragedy. I want to feel a little sorry for them even though they're scary. Give them the same details as the hero."

**A Sidekick:**
> Prompt: "Create a funny but loyal sidekick for the hero. They have a small but useful power, they're very different in personality from the hero, and they have a quirky habit."

---

## Step 5: Write the History

Even fictional worlds have history. This is what makes them feel REAL.

> Prompt: "Write a short history of the world of Emberfall. What happened 500 years ago? Who built the kingdoms? Was there ever peace? What caused the current war? Keep it short — like a legend someone might tell around a campfire."

> Prompt: "What's the most famous event in Emberfall's history? A great battle, a betrayal, a disaster? Write it like a legend."

---

## Future City Design

Want to build a city instead of a whole world? Here's how:

> Prompt: "Help me design a city 200 years in the future. It's still on Earth, but everything has changed. Give me:
> - How people travel (no cars?)
> - What the buildings look like
> - How people get food and water
> - What school looks like
> - What entertainment is like
> - One major problem the city still has, even with all the technology"

> Prompt: "What would a playground look like in this future city for a 10-year-old?"

---

## Turn Your World Into a Presentation

Here's a fun way to show off your world: build a presentation or "world guide."

Ask AI to help you write each section:

> Prompt: "I've created a world called Emberfall. Help me write a 'World Guide' that I could share with friends. It should have these sections: Overview, The Three Kingdoms, Key Characters, Important Places, and The Conflict. Keep each section short and exciting — like the back of a book."

You could then:
- Create slides (use Google Slides, Canva, or PowerPoint)
- Add drawings or AI-generated images
- Present it to your family or class

---

## Game World Design

If you love video games, design your OWN game world:

> Prompt: "Help me design a world for a side-scrolling adventure game. The hero is a small robot trying to find their lost memory chips in a broken-down city. Design:
> - 3 different levels (each with a different environment and challenge)
> - 2 types of enemies per level
> - 1 boss at the end
> - 3 power-ups the player can find
> - The final boss and how you defeat them"

You don't need to know how to code. You're designing it — like a game designer. If you ever learn to code later, you'd have the whole design ready to go!

## Exercises

- [ ] Pick a world type (fantasy, sci-fi, future city, game, etc.) and give your world a name.
- [ ] Ask AI to help you design the 3 core elements: landscape, people, and a central conflict.
- [ ] Create at least one main character for your world with a full backstory.
- [ ] Ask AI to describe your world so you can draw a simple map or sketch.

---

## Homework

Build a complete "World Introduction" document for your world.

It should include:

1. **World Name and Type** — What is this place?
2. **Overview** — 3–5 sentences describing the world.
3. **Map Description** — What does the land look like? (Bonus: draw a rough map!)
4. **One Main Character** — Name, personality, backstory, goal, and fear.
5. **The Big Problem** — What conflict, danger, or mystery drives the story?

Use AI to help write all of these sections, then edit them to sound like YOU.

Once you're done — you're a world builder! 🌍
