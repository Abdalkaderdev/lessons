# Ali's Curriculum

## About Ali

**Age:** 20
**Starting point:** Familiar with ChatGPT, zero formal AI or business background
**Goal:** Use AI as a practical business tool — for research, writing, data analysis, building an online presence, and running a more productive operation

---

## Curriculum Philosophy

Every lesson is hands-on and immediately applicable. Ali should be able to finish a lesson and use what was covered the same day in a real business context. No theory for its own sake. No coding required. Just AI tools applied to real business problems.

---
