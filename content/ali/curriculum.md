# Ali's Curriculum

## About Ali

**Age:** 20
**Starting point:** Familiar with ChatGPT, zero formal AI or business background
**Goal:** Use AI as a practical business tool — for research, writing, data analysis, building an online presence, and running a more productive operation

---

## Curriculum Philosophy

Every lesson is hands-on and immediately applicable. Ali should be able to finish a lesson and use what was covered the same day in a real business context. No theory for its own sake. No coding required. Just AI tools applied to real business problems.

---

## Module Overview

| Module | Lessons | Focus |
|--------|---------|-------|
| AI for Business | 4 | Market research, business plans, documents, operations |
| AI for Writing | 3 | Emails, reports & proposals, presentations |
| AI for Data | 3 | Spreadsheets, data analysis, financial analysis |
| AI for Websites | 3 | Building sites, deployment, e-commerce & SEO |
| AI for Productivity | 3 | Task management, decision making, automation |

**Total: 16 lessons**

## Module 1: AI for Business

*Using AI as a business strategy and operations tool*

### Lesson 1 — Market Research with AI
`content/ali/ai-for-business/01-market-research.md`

Topics: Competitor analysis, market sizing (TAM/SAM/SOM), trend identification, customer personas, SWOT analysis with AI.
Outcome: Complete market research for a real business idea.

### Lesson 2 — Business Plans & Strategy
`content/ali/ai-for-business/02-business-plans.md`

Topics: Business plan structure, drafting each section with AI, executive summaries, mission/vision statements, financial projections, pitch deck outlines.
Outcome: A full business plan first draft.

### Lesson 3 — Business Documents with AI
`content/ali/ai-for-business/03-business-documents.md`

Topics: Service contracts, project proposals, Statements of Work (SOW), Memorandums of Understanding (MOU), professional invoices, legal language basics, reusable templates.
Outcome: A complete document starter kit.

### Lesson 4 — Business Structure & Operations
`content/ali/ai-for-business/04-business-structure.md`

Topics: Organizational charts, Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs), workflow documentation, process optimization with LEAN principles, employee handbooks, meeting agendas and minutes.
Outcome: Operational documentation for a real or planned business.

## Module 3: AI for Data

*Using AI to understand and work with business data*

### Lesson 1 — Spreadsheets with AI
`content/ali/ai-for-data/01-spreadsheets-with-ai.md`

Topics: Getting formulas from AI (VLOOKUP, IF, SUMIF, date formulas, text formulas), data cleaning, pivot tables, solving real spreadsheet problems.
Outcome: A working business spreadsheet with AI-generated formulas.

### Lesson 2 — Data Analysis & Insights
`content/ali/ai-for-data/02-data-analysis.md`

Topics: Uploading data to Claude, finding patterns and trends, identifying outliers, chart and visualization recommendations, making data-driven decisions, building a business dashboard.
Outcome: A data insights report on real or generated business data.

### Lesson 3 — Financial Analysis
`content/ali/ai-for-data/03-financial-analysis.md`

Topics: Reading and analyzing P&L statements, budget planning and tracking, revenue projections (3 scenarios), break-even analysis, key financial metrics (CAC, CLV, runway), basic financial modeling.
Outcome: A 3-year financial model and a financial summary document.

## Module 4: AI for Websites

`content/ali/ai-for-websites/01-build-with-ai.md`

Topics: v0.dev and Claude for website generation, anatomy of a landing page, writing effective website copy, iterating with follow-up prompts, no-code website creation.

## Module 5: AI for Productivity

*Using AI to work smarter, decide better, and automate the rest*

### Lesson 1 — Task & Project Management
`content/ali/ai-for-productivity/01-task-management.md`

Topics: Project decomposition, Eisenhower Matrix, MoSCoW method, 1-3-5 rule, weekly planning, delegation frameworks, complete project planning process.
Outcome: A full project plan and weekly planning system.

### Lesson 2 — Decision Making with AI
`content/ali/ai-for-productivity/02-decision-making.md`

Topics: Pros/cons analysis with weighting, decision matrices, risk assessment framework, AI as devil's advocate, 10-10-10 framework, scenario planning, reversibility test.
Outcome: A structured decision-making process for any major business choice.

### Lesson 3 — Automation & AI Workflows
`content/ali/ai-for-productivity/03-automation-and-workflows.md`

Topics: Identifying automation candidates, Zapier basics, Make.com introduction, email automation sequences, building AI workflows (form → AI → email), full business automation audit.
Outcome: At least 2 working automations and a 90-day automation roadmap.

## Completion Milestones

By the end of each module, Ali will have:

**After Module 1:** A complete business plan, document starter kit, and operational system for a real or planned business.

**After Module 2:** Professional writing templates for every major business communication type.

**After Module 3:** The ability to analyze any financial or business dataset and make data-driven decisions.

**After Module 4:** A live website with e-commerce capability, SEO foundation, and Google Business Profile.

**After Module 5:** A productivity system, decision-making framework, and automation pipeline running in the background.

**After all 5 modules:** Ali operates like a one-person business team — with AI handling research, writing, data, web presence, and operations support.
